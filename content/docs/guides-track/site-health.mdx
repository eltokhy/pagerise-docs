**Track → Site Health** covers the technical foundation of a site, grouped into
indexation, performance, and structure.

## Indexation

- **URL Inspection** — Google's index status for URLs (via GSC).
- **Monitoring** *(Pro)* — index-coverage trends over time.
- **IndexNow** — submit URLs to IndexNow and track submission status for faster
  discovery.

## Performance

- **Web Vitals** — Core Web Vitals (LCP, CLS, and friends).
- **Sitemap** — sitemap presence and structure.

## Structure

- **Canonical** — canonical-tag audit.
- **Hreflang** — multi-region/language tagging.
- **Schema** — structured data (JSON-LD) coverage and validity.
- **Robots.txt** — robots validation, including whether AI crawlers
  (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, …) are allowed.
- **Site-wide** — crawl-level blockers and warnings.

<Callout type="info">
  The **Robots.txt** and **Schema** checks feed directly into
  [AI Visibility](/docs/guides-track/ai-visibility): blocking AI crawlers or
  missing schema both suppress AI citations.
</Callout>

**Report → Reports** generates client-ready reports for a site and lists past
ones. Two report types cover most agency needs.

## Monthly performance report

A rollup of the month's results, pulled from your connected data:

- **Search performance** from GSC — clicks, impressions, CTR, average position.
- **Ranking movements** — keywords that improved or declined.
- **Traffic** from GA4 — sessions, users, conversions, organic trend.
- **Content published** — the editorial pieces that went live, plus a separate
  count of **local landing pages** published.

## AI-visibility report

A shareable report of the site's [AI Visibility](/docs/guides-track/ai-visibility)
audit — the score, per-engine coverage, findings, and the recommended playbook.

## Generating reports

Generate a report on demand from the Reports page, or let scheduled monthly
reports run automatically. Each report is saved as a snapshot you can revisit or
share.

<Callout type="info">
  Reports read whatever data is connected. Connect GSC + GA4 for the richest
  monthly report; run an AI-visibility audit before generating its report.
</Callout>

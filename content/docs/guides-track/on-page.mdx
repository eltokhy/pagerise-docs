title: On-Page
description: On-page audits across the site — titles, meta descriptions, headings, images, Open Graph, internal links, and near-duplicate detection.
---

**Track → On-Page** audits the content quality of every crawled page and rolls it
into a score, with drill-downs per dimension.

## What it checks

- **Score** — an overall content-quality scorecard for the site.
- **Titles** — presence, length, and quality of `<title>` tags.
- **Meta** — meta descriptions (missing, too short/long, duplicated).
- **Headings** — H1 presence and heading structure.
- **Images** — alt text and image hygiene.
- **OpenGraph** — OG tags for social/link previews.
- **Internal Links** — anchor text and link targets.
- **Similarity** *(Pro)* — near-duplicate page detection.

Each dimension lists the affected pages so you can fix the worst offenders first;
a **page detail** view shows everything flagged on a single URL.

<Callout type="info">
  On-Page reads the latest crawl snapshot. Re-crawl after edits to refresh the
  findings. Many on-page issues also appear in
  [Tasks](/docs/guides-run/overview-and-tasks) with one-click fixes.
</Callout>

---
title: Glossary
description: Key terms used across Pagerise.
---

**AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)** — optimizing a site to be cited by AI
assistants (ChatGPT, Perplexity, AI Overviews), not just ranked by Google.

**AI Visibility** — Pagerise's measure of how likely AI engines are to surface and
cite a site. See [AI Visibility](/docs/guides-track/ai-visibility).

**Brain** — a client/workspace knowledge base of real expertise that grounds
AI-generated content. See [Brain](/docs/guides-run/brain).

**Brief** — an editorial plan for a piece of content (intent, outline, keywords,
questions) that a draft is generated from.

**Client** — a business you do work for; owns one or more sites.

**Crawl / snapshot** — a fetch of a site's pages and the point-in-time record it
produces, which audits read from.

**GBP** — Google Business Profile, the business's Maps/local-pack listing.

**GSC / GA4** — Google Search Console (search performance) and Google Analytics 4
(traffic), the main connected data sources.

**Local Pages** — bulk service × location landing pages with local schema. See
[Local Pages](/docs/guides-run/local-pages).

**NAP** — Name, Address, Phone — the consistent local identity signals AI and
local search rely on.

**Site** — a single website (domain); the level most tools operate at.

**Skill** — a one-click AI task run against a crawl. See
[Skills](/docs/guides-run/skills).

**Snapshot (report)** — a saved, shareable report generated at a point in time.

**Workspace (organization)** — your agency account; holds clients, sites, billing,
and team.

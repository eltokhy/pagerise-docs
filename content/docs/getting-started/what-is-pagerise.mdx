title: What is Pagerise?
description: An agency platform for SEO and AI visibility — crawl, audit, create, publish, track, and report across all your clients' sites.
---

Pagerise is a workspace where an agency manages SEO and **AI visibility** for many
clients at once. From one place you can crawl and audit a client's site, plan and
write content, generate local landing pages, publish to WordPress, track rankings
and AI citations, and send branded monthly reports.

## Who it's for

- **Agencies and freelancers** running SEO for multiple clients.
- **In-house teams** managing one or several brands.

Work is organized so each client's data, content, and reports stay separate while
you operate everything from a single login.

## How the app is organized

Pagerise has two "shells" — the chrome around your work changes depending on where
you are:

- **Org shell** — the workspace level: your roster of **clients** and **sites**,
  billing, costs, notifications, and workspace settings.
- **Per-site shell** — everything for one site, grouped into four areas you'll see
  in the left nav:
  - **Run** — create and act: Overview & Tasks, Content, Local Pages, Google
    Business, Brain, Skills.
  - **Track** — audit and monitor: Search Insights, On-Page, Site Health, Links,
    AI Visibility.
  - **Report** — client-facing reports and sharing.
  - **Settings** — connections and configuration for that site.

<Callout type="info">
  The documentation sidebar mirrors this exact structure, so a tool you're looking
  at in the app maps directly to its guide here.
</Callout>

## What makes it different

Pagerise treats **AI search** as a first-class channel: it scores how likely
ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, and Copilot are to cite a site,
generates schema-rich local pages built for AI answers, and reports AI visibility
alongside traditional rankings.

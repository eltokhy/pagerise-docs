---
title: Sharing & branding
description: Share reports via public links (optionally password-protected) and white-label them with the client's or your agency's branding.
---

Reports are meant to be sent. Pagerise gives every report a shareable link and
applies your branding so it looks like your agency's (or the client's) own.

## Public share links

Each report can be shared via a public URL — the client doesn't need a Pagerise
login. Share links support:

- **Password protection** — gate the report behind a password.
- **Expiry** — links can expire after a set time.
- **Revocation** — revoke a link at any time to cut off access.

## Branding

Reports pick up branding from your workspace and per-site overrides — logo,
primary/accent colors, and a report footer — so a shared report is white-labeled.
Configure this in workspace **Settings** and per site under
[Settings → Branding](/docs/settings/site-settings).

<Callout type="info">
  Set branding once at the workspace level; override per client/site only when a
  client needs their own logo and colors on the report.
</Callout>

**Run → Google Business** manages the client's **Google Business Profile (GBP)** —
the listing that shows in Maps and the local pack.

## What you can do

- **Connect a location** — link the GBP location in **Settings → Google accounts**,
  then pick it under Google Business → Settings.
- **Compose posts** — create GBP posts (updates, events, offers) with scheduling.
- **Schedule** — queue posts for a future date; Pagerise publishes them when due.
- **Track activity** — see recent posts and any publishing errors.

## Why GBP matters for AI visibility

GBP synchronization and consistent local signals correlate strongly with how
often AI assistants surface a local business. Keeping the profile active — fresh
posts, accurate NAP — reinforces the same local signals that
[Local Pages](/docs/guides-run/local-pages) and local schema provide on-site.

<Callout type="info">
  Connecting GBP requires the Google account that manages the listing. See
  [Settings → Integrations](/docs/settings/integrations).
</Callout>

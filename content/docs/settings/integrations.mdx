Integrations connect the external data and publishing channels that power
Pagerise. Most live under a site's **Settings → Google accounts** and
**Settings → Integrations**; a few are workspace-level.

## WordPress

Publish content and apply one-click fixes to the live site. Connect with the
**one-click plugin install**, or manually with a WordPress **application
password**. Once connected, you get a health badge and can publish drafts, set
featured images, and inject schema directly.

## Google Search Console (GSC)

Powers [Search Insights](/docs/guides-track/search-insights) and URL inspection.
Connect the Google account with access to the property.

## Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

Traffic, conversions, and **AI-assistant referrers** for reports and the
AI-visibility referrers view.

## Google Business Profile (GBP)

Post to and manage the client's listing — see
[Google Business](/docs/guides-run/google-business).

## DataForSEO *(bring your own key)*

An optional workspace-level provider for premium SERP/keyword data. Add your API
key in workspace settings to enable richer keyword research.

## MCP

Programmatic access via the Model Context Protocol for advanced/automation use.

<Callout type="info">
  You can run Pagerise with just a crawl — but connecting GSC + GA4 + WordPress
  unlocks Search Insights, AI-referrer data, richer reports, and one-click
  publishing/fixes.
</Callout>

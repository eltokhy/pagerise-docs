## My fixes / edits aren't reflected in the audit

Audits and On-Page read the **latest crawl snapshot**. Re-crawl the site after
shipping changes so scores and [Tasks](/docs/guides-run/overview-and-tasks) update.

## Search Insights is empty

It needs **Google Search Console** connected, and GSC data lags ~2 days. Connect
GSC in [Settings → Integrations](/docs/settings/integrations) and check back.

## A published page is missing its schema

Schema is delivered through the WordPress plugin (post meta → `wp_head`), not the
post body — WordPress strips inline `<script>` for non-`unfiltered_html` users.
Confirm the plugin is connected and healthy in
[Integrations](/docs/settings/integrations).

## AI assistants aren't citing the site

Start with [Site Health → Robots](/docs/guides-track/site-health) — blocked AI
crawlers stop everything. Then work the
[Improve AI visibility](/docs/playbooks/improve-ai-visibility) playbook.

## Local Pages are stuck in "Needs review"

That's the thin-content / near-duplicate guard. Make the flagged pages more
distinct (vary the copy, add location-specific detail) — they can't publish until
they pass. See [Local Pages](/docs/guides-run/local-pages).

## A background job seems stuck

Most jobs finish in seconds to minutes. If one looks stuck, give it a few minutes;
sets/runs that are interrupted by a restart are reconciled automatically.

<Callout type="info">
  Still stuck? Contact support from inside the app — include the site and the
  approximate time so we can find the job.
</Callout>

---
title: Brain
description: The client knowledge base that grounds AI-generated content in real expertise, methodology, and results.
---

The **Brain** is a knowledge base of a client's real expertise — methodology,
results, data, named credentials, differentiators, and brand facts. Pagerise
injects relevant Brain documents into content generation so drafts reflect the
client's true E-E-A-T instead of generic, hallucinated filler.

## How it works

- Add documents at the **site/client** level (specific to one client) or at the
  **workspace** level (shared facts that apply everywhere).
- When you generate a brief, draft, or local page, Pagerise automatically pulls
  the relevant Brain documents (client-specific first, then workspace-wide) within
  a token budget, and grounds the writing in them.

## What to put in it

- The client's process and what makes it different.
- Real, citable results and data (with the specifics you're allowed to publish).
- Credentials, awards, certifications, service areas.
- Brand voice notes and things to never say.

<Callout type="info">
  The Brain is the single biggest lever on content quality. A few paragraphs of
  real client facts turn generic drafts into copy only that client could publish.
</Callout>

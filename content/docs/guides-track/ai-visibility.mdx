**Track → AI Visibility** scores how likely AI assistants are to surface and cite
a site, and tracks the traffic they send. It's the AI-search counterpart to
[Search Insights](/docs/guides-track/search-insights).

## What it measures

When you run an audit, Pagerise probes a set of high-intent queries across five
engines — **ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, and Copilot** — and
produces an **AI Visibility score (0–100)** from:

- **Query coverage** — how many queries the brand is surfaced for.
- **Engine coverage** — how dominant it is on its strongest AI channel.
- **Schema** — structured-data signals AI engines use to extract answers.
- **Citations** — presence on authority directories AI engines trust.
- **Local signals** — NAP, hours, and local context.

The audit also returns an executive summary and prioritized findings (a playbook
of what to fix next).

## The three drill-downs

- **Verdicts** — a per-query, per-engine matrix: for each query, was the brand
  **found / weak / miss** on each engine, with the reasoning.
- **Citations** — the authority directories detected vs. missing (the citation
  gap to close).
- **Referrers** — real GA4 traffic arriving from AI assistants (ChatGPT,
  Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, …).

## How to lift it

<Steps>
<Step>
### Unblock AI crawlers
Make sure robots.txt doesn't block GPTBot/ClaudeBot/PerplexityBot — check
[Site Health → Robots](/docs/guides-track/site-health).
</Step>
<Step>
### Ship schema + local signals
Add LocalBusiness/Service/FAQ schema and consistent NAP. Bulk-generate service-area
pages with [Local Pages](/docs/guides-run/local-pages).
</Step>
<Step>
### Close the citation gap
Get listed on the authority directories the Citations view flags as missing.
</Step>
<Step>
### Publish depth
Cover the high-intent queries that came back **weak/miss** with focused content.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
  For a deeper one-off analysis (citability, brand mentions, llms.txt), run the
  **AI Visibility Skill** — see [Skills](/docs/guides-run/skills).
</Callout>

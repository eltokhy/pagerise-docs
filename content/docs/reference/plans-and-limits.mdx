Pagerise has a free tier plus paid plans. Higher plans unlock **Pro** tools and
raise usage limits.

## Free vs. Pro tools

Most core tools are available on every plan: Overview & Tasks, On-Page audits,
Site Health basics, Content, Skills, and AI Visibility.

**Pro** tools include:

- **Topics** — semantic keyword/topic research.
- **Similarity** — near-duplicate page detection.
- **Index Monitoring** — index-coverage trends over time.
- **Link Suggestions** and **Backlinks**.

Pro features are labeled in the app and in these docs.

## Usage quota

AI actions — content generation, Skills, and AI-visibility audits — draw from your
workspace's monthly quota. Track consumption in **Costs**.

## Managing your plan

Upgrade, downgrade, and see invoices in **Billing**. See
[Billing & plans](/docs/settings/billing-and-plans).

<Callout type="warning">
  Exact tier names, prices, and numeric limits are shown live in **Billing** —
  this page describes the model, not the current price sheet.
</Callout>

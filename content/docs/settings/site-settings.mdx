title: Site settings
description: Per-site configuration — general details, crawl behavior, page management, users, branding, and notifications.
---

Each site has its own settings, reached from **Settings** inside the per-site
shell.

## General

Site name, normalized URL, crawl frequency, and timezone.

## Crawl

Control how Pagerise crawls: depth, speed, include/exclude (allow/block) rules,
custom headers, and user agent. Tune this when a site is large, slow, or has
sections you don't want analyzed.

## Manage pages

Exclude specific pages from crawling, auditing, and tracking — useful for staging
paths, thank-you pages, or anything that shouldn't count in scores.

## Users

Site-level team access — who on your workspace can work on this site.

## Branding

Per-site logo, colors, and report footer that override the workspace defaults on
this client's reports. See [Sharing & branding](/docs/guides-report/sharing-and-branding).

## Notifications

Email alerts and Discord/Telegram webhooks for events like crawl completion,
audit issues, and publishing.

<Callout type="info">
  Most teams set workspace branding + notification defaults once, then only touch
  per-site settings for exceptions.
</Callout>

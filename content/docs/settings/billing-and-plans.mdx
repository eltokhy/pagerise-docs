Billing is at the **workspace** level.

## Billing

In **Billing**, manage your subscription tier, view invoice history and payment
methods, and upgrade or downgrade. Billing is handled through Stripe.

## Plans

Higher tiers unlock Pro tools (deeper keyword research like **Topics**,
**Similarity**, index **Monitoring**, internal-link **Suggestions**, and
**Backlinks**) and higher limits. AI actions (content generation, Skills,
AI-visibility audits) draw from your workspace quota.

## Costs

The **Costs** view tracks monthly spend by service category so you can see what's
driving usage.

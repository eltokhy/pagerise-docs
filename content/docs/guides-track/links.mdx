**Track → Links** audits the site's link structure — both what's broken and where
there's opportunity.

## Audit

- **Broken** — internal/outbound links returning errors, timeouts, or bad
  redirects.
- **Orphans** — pages with no internal links pointing to them (hard for crawlers
  and users to find).
- **Redirects** — redirect chains and loops worth cleaning up.

## Advanced

- **Suggestions** *(Pro)* — internal-link opportunities: relevant pages that
  should link to each other.
- **Backlinks** *(Pro)* — the site's inbound link profile.

<Callout type="info">
  Orphan pages are a common, quiet drag on both SEO and AI discovery — fixing
  internal links so every important page is reachable is high-leverage.
</Callout>

title: Invite your team
description: Add teammates to your workspace and manage their access.
---

Pagerise is multi-user. Bring your team into the workspace so they can manage
clients and sites alongside you.

## Invite members

In **Settings** (workspace level), open the invitations panel and send an invite
by email. Pending invites can be copied as a link, resent, or revoked.

<Steps>
<Step>
### Open workspace settings
Go to **Settings** from the org-level nav.
</Step>
<Step>
### Send an invitation
Enter the teammate's email and choose their role.
</Step>
<Step>
### Share the link (optional)
Copy the invite link to send it directly if email delivery is slow.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Roles

Members have a role that controls what they can do — from full owner/admin access
down to view-only. Site-level access can also be scoped under
**Settings → Users** on an individual site.

<Callout type="info">
  Not sure who should see what? Start narrow — you can widen a member's access at
  any time.
</Callout>
